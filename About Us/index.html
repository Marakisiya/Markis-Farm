Our Stories

Markis Poultry Farm (MPF)

MPF was incorporated in November 2016 and has since then, been at the forefront of livestock in Kano, Nigeria. MPF have deployed modern technology to ensure quality poultry products in addition to excellent customer services. The farm is located along Zoo road in Kano state, Nigeria. With a capacity of over sixty-five thousand (65000) laying birds in our poultry, we are determined to contribute our quota in ensuring food security in Nigeria and creating jobs for the teaming population. MPF is driven by quality and passion to produce highest grade of quality poultry products that will enhance wholesome living. We guarantee customers poultry products of the highest quality.



Our mission

To support wholesome living through the production of globally competitive organic poultry products guided by best practices.

Our vision

To establish an innovative poultry system that would ensure the provision of organic poultry products that support wholesome living.
